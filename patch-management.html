Patch Management

Keep every device secure, updated, and compliant — with minimal effort from your team.

Overview

Patch Management is about more than just updates — it's peace of mind. We help you keep Windows, macOS, and core software up-to-date, patched against the latest vulnerabilities, and aligned with compliance expectations (especially in education or regulated industries).

What We Offer

  • Automated Device Patching: Cloud-managed updates for Windows and macOS, scheduled and monitored.
  • Light-touch Monitoring: We ensure updates run smoothly — and alert you only when needed.
  • Audit-Ready Reports: Generate proof of compliance for governors, trustees, or leadership.
  • Secure Baseline Settings: Enforce OS-level protections like BitLocker and Defender.

Why It Matters

Out-of-date systems are the #1 vector for security breaches. Patching regularly reduces risk dramatically — and lets your team focus on supporting users, not chasing updates.

Who It’s For

  • Schools who need to meet safeguarding expectations
  • SMEs with remote teams or unmanaged laptops
  • IT teams with limited time for manual patching
