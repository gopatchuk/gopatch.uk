What Is Patch Management?

Patch management is the process of keeping your systems up to date by applying operating system and software updates. At GoPatch, we handle this for you in a fully managed, low‑touch way — so your team stays protected without lifting a finger.

What We Offer

Automated Updates

We deploy patches automatically across your Windows and Mac devices to keep everything secure and up to date — without interrupting your users.

Audit & Compliance

We track patch status and provide exportable reports to help you meet audit requirements, including Cyber Essentials and other standards.

Oversight Without the Noise

We give you access to a clean dashboard (if you want it), or you can leave it to us and receive scheduled check-ins with a summary.

Why It Matters

Most cyberattacks exploit known vulnerabilities in unpatched software. With GoPatch, you don’t need to worry about falling behind. Our service reduces risk, supports compliance, and saves internal time — without complex tools or alerts.
