Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

At GoPatch, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data we collect or process. This privacy policy outlines the types of data we may collect from visitors to our website and how we use and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect certain non-personal information automatically when you visit our site, such as browser type, operating system, and pages viewed. If you choose to contact us directly, we may also collect your name, email address, and message content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide requested information.
  • To monitor website performance and improve user experience.
  • To ensure website security and prevent misuse.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to provide basic functionality and monitor usage patterns. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable laws.

5. Third Parties

We do not sell your data to third parties. We may use third-party tools to monitor site performance or send email communications. Any third-party providers are GDPR-compliant and bound by similar data protection obligations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact us at info@gopatch.uk.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please contact us at info@gopatch.uk.
